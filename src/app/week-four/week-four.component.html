Week 4

In this demonstration, there are 3 components and 1 service:

  • WeekFourComponent
  • AccountBalanceComponent
  • AccountDetailsComponent
  • TransactionService

WeekFourComponent displays the two child components, AccountBalanceComponent and AccountDetailsComponent. It also displays a button.

The TransactionService creates an Observable that both the AccountBalanceComponent and AccountDetailsComponent subscribe to. They are the Observers.

When the button in WeekFourComponent is activated, the WeekFourComponent calls the TransactionService to tell it a new purchase has been made.

When the TransactionService receives that message from WeekFourComponent, the TransactionService will push out a message to all of its Observers.

Since AccountBalanceComponent and AccountDetailsComponent are both Observers, they will receive this update from the Observable that they subscribed to.

Once they have received an update from the Observable that there is a new value, AccountBalanceComponent and AccountDetailsComponent can handle that information however they want.
